My Story

From Average to Ascending.

I used to walk into a room and feel invisible. Not because I was — but because I hadn't yet learned how to carry myself, how to present myself, or how to unlock the version of me that was already there.

That changed when I started taking looksmaxxing seriously. Not in an obsessive way — but in the same way an athlete trains. Deliberately. Consistently. With intention.

On @chigitn, I document the exact methods I used: skincare, grooming, fitness, posture, style, and mindset. Everything I wish someone had told me earlier. No gatekeeping. All signal, no noise.
